About KnotSpot

KnotSpot is a completely independent social platform designed to help you connect, socialize, find roommates, and interact safely across SRM campuses.

Open App View Confessions

College is massive, and it's easy to feel lost in the crowd. We noticed how hard it was to just meet people outside our immediate circles, find reliable roommates without getting scammed, or share thoughts freely without judgment.

So we built KnotSpot. It's not a corporate product—it's a genuine attempt to make campus life a little more connected, a little safer, and a lot more fun.
