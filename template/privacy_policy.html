Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

1. Data Collection and Use

KnotSpot collects limited information necessary to operate and protect the platform.

This may include:

  • Account information
  • Profile information
  • Uploaded images
  • Verification images
  • Quiz answers and preferences
  • Chat and interaction data
  • Roommate listing data
  • Confession and moderation data
  • Device, browser, fingerprint, IP, or security-related identifiers

This information may be used to:

  • Provide app features
  • Improve matching and recommendations
  • Verify accounts
  • Prevent fake accounts and abuse
  • Investigate reports
  • Improve safety and platform performance

KnotSpot does not sell your personal data.

2. Private and Public Content

Some areas of KnotSpot may be publicly accessible for landing pages, approved confessions, public community features, or PWA installation support.

Private information such as verification images, private chats, hidden moderation data, and account security information is not intentionally displayed publicly.

Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal information through public or semi-public features.
